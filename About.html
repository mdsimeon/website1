Student Services Department


The Student Services department is charged with the responsibility of providing comprehensive programs and services to promote the academic functioning and well being of all students. Student services personel work in conjuction with other educators, parents/guardians, and community organizations supporting the academic and social emotional needs of all students, throughout their college journey and assist each student to prepare for their lives after leaving the institution. The primary functions of the Student Services department include:

  • College and career services
  • Health services (Nurses' office)
  • Mental health services (Social Workers, Psychologists and Counselors)
  • School counseling
  • School registration
  • Student records
  • Substance use prevention, intervention and assistance
  • Transcripts
  • Graduation requirements
  • Transfers
